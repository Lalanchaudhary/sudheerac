FAQ

Common Questions

How often should AC be serviced?

For regular home use, service your AC at least twice a year, ideally before and during peak summer.

What is included in gas filling?

We inspect for leaks, check pressure levels, refill refrigerant as required, and test cooling performance.

Do you provide warranty?

Yes, warranty is provided on eligible repairs and parts based on the service type and spare part used.

Is same-day service available?

Yes, same-day service is available in most areas depending on technician availability and booking time.

Which brands do you repair?

We service major brands including Daikin, LG, Samsung, Voltas, Blue Star, Hitachi, Carrier, Panasonic, Whirlpool, and Lloyd.

Do you install new ACs?

Yes, we install new split and window AC units with proper mounting, wiring checks, and final testing.
